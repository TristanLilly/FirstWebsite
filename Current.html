Halo Infinite

Halo Infinte is another game in the Halo series. We are again playing as the iconic Master Cheif going up against his most merciless oponent yet. In addition to the campaign there is also a free multiplayer for Halo Infinite. Which features customization of armor, weapons, and vehicles. The multiplayer also has a battle pass system that once purchased you never lose. Halo Infinite released on 12/08/2021. So far on steam there are roughly 100,000 reviews and 79% of them are positive.
