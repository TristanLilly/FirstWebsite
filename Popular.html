Halo Infinite

Halo Infinte is another game in the Halo series. We are again playing as the iconic Master Cheif going up against his most merciless oponent yet. In addition to the campaign there is also a free multiplayer for Halo Infinite. Which features customization of armor, weapons, and vehicles. The multiplayer also has a battle pass system that once purchased you never lose. Halo Infinite released on 12/08/2021. So far on steam there are roughly 100,000 reviews and 79% of them are positive.

Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six Siege

Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six Siege was released in 2015 by Ubisoft and is still being played and updated today. Rainbow Six Siege is a highly competetive first person shooter where two teams are pitted against one another. each team takes turns defending and attacking. Rainbow Six Siege spices up their gameplay with gadgets and operator specific weapons and equipment. Each operator has a unique gadet for their respective side.Whether that is defense or attack. Along with that each operator has their own primary and secondary weapons. So choosing operators can make or break games. Rainbow six siege continues to mix up their gameplay by routinely adding new operators with new gadgets for a new way to attack and defend. Currently Rainbow Six Siege has roughly 850,000 reviews with a mostly positive rating. In addition to that the game is frequently on sale and has free to play weekends often.
