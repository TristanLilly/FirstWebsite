Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six Extraction

Rainbow Six Extraction is similar to the another title from ubisoft Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six Seige, however this time you wont be facing other players. You will be working together with squads of three or going into the frey alone to face off against an alien threat. As of right now there are 18 operators to choose from, twelve different maps, and 10+ different alien types to fight. Rainbow Six Extraction is set to release 01/20/2022 and Ubisoft has reduced the price since the games announcement from $59.99 down to $39.99 for the standard eddition.
